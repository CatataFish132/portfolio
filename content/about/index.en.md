As a Computer Vision Engineer, I occupy a unique spot. By combining the hands-on, practical foundation of an **HBO Bachelor’s** with the analytical research depth of a **WO Master’s**, I bridge the gap between complex theory and real-world execution.

My real strength lies in **rapid prototyping**, I have a natural knack for taking an abstract concept and quickly turning it into a working reality. By moving rapidly from idea to prototype, I allow companies to try out fresh, ambitious concepts and validate them early, without needing to commit massive development resources upfront. Rather than sticking to rigid blueprints, I rely on my intuition to find creative, out-of-the-box solutions that actually work.

I am highly drawn to **R&D environments** where I can tackle ambiguous, unsolved problems that require creative problem-solving. I love working at the intersection of:

- **Computer Vision & Robotics:** Bringing machines to life by giving them spatial awareness and intelligence.
- **A Hybrid Technical Approach:** Utilizing the speed and determinism of classical image processing alongside the power of modern Deep Learning models.

My passion for technology doesn't turn off at 5:00 PM. I am a natural tinkerer who loves learning on the fly. In my free time, you will usually find me playing around with **Linux**, hosting things on my **Raspberry Pis**, or calibrating and printing on my **3D printer**.

- **Programming Languages:** Python, C/C++, Rust, Julia
